About me:

"My name is Michelle Mohr and I am lucky enough to teach fourth grade at St. Joseph School. This will be my seventeenth year teaching and I am so excited to meet my new students and explore fourth grade with them! I was born and raised in Juneau, Alaska. I have a Bachelor's degree in psychology and religion from University of Puget Sound and my Master's degree in Teaching from City University. In 1996 I began my teaching career with the Clark County School District in Las Vegas as a first grade teacher. In 2001 I moved back to WA and taught three years in the Highline School District in second grade and as a reading specialist. I worked for the Lake Washington School District several years and for the past four years I have been at St. Joseph School. My son graduated from St. Joseph School last year. I enjoy reading, walking my dogs-Cooper and Lily , and spending time with family and friends. Please look forward to this school year and all the joy and inspiration it will bring! ."
